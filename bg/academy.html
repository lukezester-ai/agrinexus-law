From lessons to operations

Academy explains why — Platform runs how

The Academy is for literacy: readings, video paths, and the Tutor. The Platform page is the live map of the same Sense → Think → Act stack.

  • Sense — market & satellite context, AgriDirect desk, field reports
  • Think — agent mesh, data lake, and model literacy (tied back to courses here)
  • Act — daily briefings, playbooks, and mobile / voice hand-offs
