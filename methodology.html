How we estimate confidence

Each forecast combines independent signals: delayed CBOT direction, news flow, weather indices for key regions, and FX (EUR/USD). Each signal has a direction and a weight.

The confidence reading reflects how many signals point the same way and how strong each is. When most align, we show strong signal; when they conflict, mixed or weak — openly, not hidden.

Important: strong alignment today does not mean the market must follow. Agricultural markets react to surprises. The indicator supports judgment; it is not a promise.

This is a heuristic based on current signal alignment. We are collecting historical data to calibrate against outcomes — treat it as orientation, not a precise probability.
